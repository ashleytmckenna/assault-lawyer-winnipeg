​Our Comprehensive Assault Defence Services 

​​​​At Wiebe Criminal Defence, we offer a wide range of services for clients facing assault charges. Our legal team is experienced in handling various types of assault cases, including: 

​Simple Assault​

​​​Simple assault refers to cases involving physical contact or threats of harm that do not result in serious injury. This can include minor altercations where no weapons are involved, and the injuries, if any, are minimal. It’s one of the most basic assault charges, though still serious, and often stems from misunderstandings or disputes that escalate. 

​​Domestic Assault 

​​Domestic assault involves accusations of physical violence or threats between individuals in a domestic relationship, such as spouses, partners, or family members. These cases are unique because they not only involve criminal law but can also have significant personal and familial consequences. Domestic assault charges can sometimes include additional factors, such as protection orders. 

​Aggravated Assault 

​​Aggravated assault is a more severe charge, involving situations where the accused is alleged to have caused significant bodily harm or used a weapon in the assault. This charge is typically applied when the injuries are serious, potentially life-threatening, or when the assault demonstrates a high level of violence. Legal penalties for aggravated assault are more substantial due to the nature of the harm caused. 

​Assault with a Weapon 

​This charge involves an assault where a weapon—whether a firearm, knife, or any object used to cause harm or threaten harm—is alleged to have been used during the incident. The involvement of a weapon often leads to enhanced charges and more severe potential penalties, even if the victim did not sustain significant injuries. 

​Assault Causing Bodily Harm 

​Assault causing bodily harm refers to incidents where physical harm is inflicted, and the injuries require medical attention but are not life-threatening. This charge falls between simple assault and aggravated assault in terms of severity. Injuries in these cases typically include cuts, bruises, or fractures. 

​Sexual Assault 

​Sexual assault involves allegations of unwanted sexual contact or behaviour, which can range from touching to more serious forms of sexual interference. These cases are highly sensitive and often require a careful and thorough legal process. The charge encompasses a wide range of actions, all of which are taken very seriously under the law.
